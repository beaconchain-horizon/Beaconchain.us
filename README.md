## 🚀 Horizon Hub – The Upcoming Marketplace

**Horizon Hub** will be a community‑driven, low‑fee platform where users can:

- ✅ Find **jobs** and hire freelancers (blockchain, development, design, etc.)
- ✅ Buy and sell **digital goods** (templates, e‑books, software licenses, APIs)
- ✅ Offer **services** with crypto payments (ETH / USDC / stablecoins)
- ✅ Earn **commissions** by inviting others and building reputation

> 🔐 **Built with privacy and decentralization in mind** – no mandatory KYC, non‑custodial payments, and open source.

## 📈 Why Horizon?

- **Independence** – Fully separated from any commercial entity since 2024.
- **Proven track record** – Years of active development, validator operation, and formal security audits.
- **Offline‑first** – Works without constant internet; cached data with digital signatures.
- **Transparent** – Every line of code is public, every commit signed with GPG.
